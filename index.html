Our Delicious Menu

At Itadaki Mas, we invite you to discover a symphony of flavors curated from the heart of Japanese cuisine. Each dish is a testament to our dedication to authenticity and innovation. From delicate appetizers to artfully crafted signature rolls, and soul-soothing noodle creations, our menu offers a captivating journey through the diverse tapestry of Japanese gastronomy. Appetizers

  • Appetizers- Steamed soybeans lightly seasoned with sea salt and a hint of citrus zest.
  • Gyoza Symphony- Delicate dumplings filled with a succulent blend of pork and vegetables, served with a tangy dipping sauce.
  • Agedashi Tofu Elegance- Crispy cubes of tofu, bathed in a savory dashi broth, topped with grated daikon and green onions.

Signature Rolls

  • Sunrise Bliss Roll- A fusion of fresh salmon, avocado, and cucumber, crowned with spicy mayo and tobiko.
  • Dragon's Embrace Roll- Eel, cucumber, and avocado, draped with thinly sliced avocado and drizzled with unagi sauce.
  • Itadaki Rainbow Roll- Tuna, salmon, and avocado elegantly wrapped in soy paper, garnished with a medley of vibrant fish roe.

Noodle Creations

  • Sizzling Yaki Udon- Thick, chewy udon noodles stir-fried with an array of colorful vegetables and your choice of protein.
  • Tan Tan Ramen Reverie- A creamy, spicy sesame broth enveloping tender ramen noodles, topped with minced pork and green onions.
  • Sakura Soba Symphony- Chilled buckwheat noodles served with a refreshing dipping sauce, accompanied by an assortment of tempura.

About Us

Nestled in the heart of the city, Itadaki Mas is a celebration of Japanese culinary artistry. Established in 2008, our restaurant was born from a shared passion for bringing the authentic flavors of Japan to discerning palates.
With a history steeped in tradition, our chefs have honed their skills under the guidance of master craftsmen in Tokyo, blending time-honored techniques with contemporary creativity. Every dish is a canvas, meticulously prepared to evoke the true essence of Japanese gastronomy.
Beyond the cuisine, Itadaki Mas exudes an ambiance of tranquility, transporting guests to the bustling streets of Tokyo. The decor, a fusion of modern elegance and subtle nods to Japanese heritage, provides a serene backdrop for an unforgettable dining experience.
Over the years, we've had the privilege of welcoming guests from all walks of life, each sharing in our passion for exceptional food and warm hospitality. At Itadaki Mas, we strive to create not just a meal, but an immersive journey through the soul of Japan.
We extend our deepest gratitude to our patrons for being an integral part of our story. It is with great pleasure that we continue to serve and share the magic of Itadaki Mas with you. Welcome, and thank you for being a part of our journey.
